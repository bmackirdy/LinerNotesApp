Product Requirements Document
Overview:

Liner Notes is a web-based application designed to help vinyl record collectors catalog, organize, and share their physical music collections in a clean, distraction-free environment.

The app focuses on the joy of collecting and preserving physical media, providing intuitive tools for users to:

Log detailed record information (artist, pressing, sleeve condition, and notes).

Track maintenance and collection updates.

Build wish lists of albums to find later.

Share collections or highlights with others who share similar interests.

Key Vision

Liner Notes is intended to be a digital companion for collectors, reflecting the authenticity and care of vinyl culture. The platform prioritizes clarity, simplicity, and usability over flashy visuals or social complexity, offering both private and community-oriented experiences.

Strategy: 
Project Goals

Deliver a simple, functional cataloging experience for all collectors.

Support both private use and optional community features (sharing, commenting, following).

Integrate Discogs API for accurate album metadata and autofill functionality.

Encourage sustainable collecting practices through organization and preservation tracking.

Create an ad-free, visually minimal interface optimized for desktop and tablet browsers.

Design Principles

Simplicity: Focus on usability and clean navigation.

Authenticity: Reflect the tactile, analog appeal of vinyl culture.

Flexibility: Allow collectors to engage privately or socially.

Functionality First: Every design choice should serve clarity and purpose.
